Hi! I'm Sejal* and I collect erasers, I said
for the fortieth time that week. My box of
erasers was my fun fact for orientation week
in college.It was the most interesting thing
about me then, and for better or for worse, it
still is(?)This website is a long overdue growing
archive of erasers I have carefully collected over
the last 15 years.They are a reminder of my
impulse to hoard and organise objects, material,
and habits.This website aims to transform these
simple erasers into objects more monumental
than their conventional use. (as they are)
